Our Story · داستان ما

Boston's Dedicated
Sitar Workshop

SitarBoston was born out of necessity and passion. As a sitar player in New England, finding skilled maintenance was nearly impossible — sitars were shipped to New York or Pennsylvania, or players waited months for help.

Trained directly by master craftsmen in Kolkata and the legendary sitar-making city of Miraj, India, we brought that expertise home. Today, Boston-area sitarists no longer need to wait, ship, or drive hours for world-class sitar care.

"No need to wait for months or ship your sitar or drive to NY or PA — professional sitar maintenance is now right here in the Boston area."

We carry concert-grade Shahid Ali sitars direct from Miraj, restore rare vintage instruments (Hiren Roy, Sarat Sardar, Kanailal), and offer personalized lessons for beginners through intermediate players — all from our workshop in Westford, MA.

Sitar Lessons

One-on-one and group lessons for adults and children — beginner through intermediate. Learn authentic technique, ragas, and the rich tradition of Hindustani classical music.

  • Adults & children welcome

Jawari & Repair

جواری و تعمیر

Expert jawari and full maintenance — the same knowledge passed down by master sitar makers of Kolkata and Miraj. VK and SPK style jawari, vintage restoration, and complete tune-ups.

  • Closed & Gol Jawari (VK / SPK style)
  • Tarab bridge jawari
  • New & replacement main bridges
  • Fret tightening & adjustment
  • Tuning peg & langot repair
  • Tar Gahan maintenance
  • Full string set (main & tarab)
  • Vintage instrument restoration
  • Accessories & supplies
Contact for pricing · in-studio or by arrangement
